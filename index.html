What I Design & Build

Protocol Engineering

I design and implement consensus-layer upgrades across multiple blockchain clients written in different languages - Go, JVM/Scala, and Java. I've coordinated a multi-client hard fork integrating over a dozen EIPs and ECIPs, discovering specification-level bugs through cross-client analysis that would have caused consensus failures in production. I run full nodes across all client implementations and build the cross-client validation infrastructure to prove behavioral equivalence across fundamentally different codebases. The work is as much about coordinating across independent engineering teams as it is about writing code.

Governance & Treasury Architecture

I architect governance and custody infrastructure for institutional digital asset environments - from non-transferable credential-based voting to DAO frameworks to prediction-market-based decision mechanisms. This includes on-chain treasury systems managing significant assets with custody isolation, separation-of-duties enforcement, and auditable execution pipelines. I also design the regulatory infrastructure layer: stablecoin reserve custody workflows, compliance pipelines aligned with emerging frameworks like the GENIUS and CLARITY Acts, and freeze-seize controls for OCC-regulated environments. Every system is backed by rigorous formal verification, with individual contract systems carrying hundreds to over a thousand tests.

Smart Contracts & Tokenization

I design and deploy smart contract systems spanning token standards (ERC-20, ERC-721, ERC-1400), tokenization workflows, and DeFi primitives - lending markets, oracle integration, liquidity provisioning, prediction markets, and stablecoin infrastructure. I've managed large-scale digital asset treasury operations and built the trading infrastructure around them: liquidity routing, settlement, and analytics across dozens of tokens. The same architect who writes the contracts also builds the TypeScript interfaces and product experiences that sit on top - from contract architecture through user interaction with no handoff gap.

Data Architecture & Infrastructure

I design pipelines that process over a billion records across multiple large-scale datasets. This involves performance-engineering storage layers to achieve order-of-magnitude throughput improvements, building multi-pass enrichment architectures, and constructing full-text search infrastructure that serves complex queries across millions of documents. The same systems thinking that governs protocol design also produces high-performance data systems.

Security and regulatory awareness are built into the process, not bolted on afterward. Adversarial modeling, formal verification, and fuzz testing are standard practice.
